About Us

Since 1975, Mikasa Sports Club has been the choice for those who want something more than just a gym membership. We’re on a mission to provide the best health club experience in the Upstate of Mukah District. A place where you will rediscover your youthful spirit. Our members appreciate a wide variety of equipment, exercise programs, clean locker rooms and an attentive staff that respects their time and space. From team sports to group activities to individual instruction, we can define a fitness program that suits anyone’s unique ability and lifestyle. We don’t just want you to join, we want to be part of the reason you’re able to achieve your healthy goals week after week, year after year!
