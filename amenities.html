Pool

There are three pools on the Mikasa Sports Club Property. The Central Pool has a water slide, four hottubs, a waterfall, and shallow shelf for small children. The River Pool is a flowing pool with side pools for relaxation.

Tennis Courts

The tennis courts are located on top of the parking garage. There are five tennis courts and one practice half court with a ball machine. Tennis balls and tennis lesson registration is available at the Sports Club activities desk.

Gym

The work out room at the Mikasa overlooks the ocean. Exercise using treadmills, elliptical machines, stationary bicycles, weight machines, and free weights while enjoying a view of the beautiful white sand beach. Rise and shine exercise programs begin at 8 am with pool aerobics and beach yoga.

Game Room

Video games, interactive games, a bowling lane, and shooting gallery are the main source of entertainment. Pool tables and ping pong tables are available for hourly rental.
